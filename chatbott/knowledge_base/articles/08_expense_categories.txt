EXPENSE CATEGORIES SETUP

Purpose: Configure expense categories with default GST parameters.

Navigation Path: Setup > Accounting > Expense Categories

CONFIGURABLE FIELDS:

1. Nature of Item: Goods or Services classification
2. HSN/SAC Code: Applicable classification code
3. RCM Applicable: Reverse Charge Mechanism flag

BENEFIT: 
Streamlines expense entry by pre-populating tax details.

WHAT ARE EXPENSE CATEGORIES:

Expense categories are used in:
- Expense Reports
- Employee reimbursements
- Credit card expenses
- Vendor bills (optional)

Pre-configuring GST details ensures:
- Consistent tax treatment
- Accurate ITC claims
- Faster expense processing
- Compliance with GST rules

COMMON EXPENSE CATEGORIES:

Travel Expenses:
├── Nature: Services
├── SAC: 996411 (Transportation services)
└── RCM: No

Hotel/Accommodation:
├── Nature: Services
├── SAC: 996311 (Accommodation services)
└── RCM: No

Food & Beverages:
├── Nature: Services
├── SAC: 996331 (Restaurant services)
└── RCM: No (but may be ineligible ITC)

Professional Fees:
├── Nature: Services
├── SAC: 998313 (Legal services) or similar
└── RCM: May be Yes (if unregistered vendor)

Office Supplies:
├── Nature: Goods
├── HSN: Varies by item
└── RCM: No

Rent:
├── Nature: Services
├── SAC: 997212 (Rental services)
└── RCM: May be Yes

CONFIGURATION STEPS:

1. Navigate to Expense Categories:
   Setup > Accounting > Expense Categories

2. Select or Create Category:
   - Click on existing category to edit
   - Or click "New" to create new

3. Configure GST Fields:
   - Category Name: Clear identifier
   - Nature of Item: Goods/Services
   - HSN/SAC Code: Appropriate code
   - RCM Applicable: Check if applicable
   - Default Account: GL account

4. Save Configuration:
   - Click Save
   - Test with expense report

EXPENSE REPORT WORKFLOW WITH GST:

1. Employee creates expense report
2. Selects pre-configured expense category
3. GST fields auto-populate from category setup
4. Employee adds receipt and submits
5. Finance reviews and approves
6. System posts with correct GST treatment

IMPORTANT CONSIDERATIONS:

Ineligible ITC on Expenses:
- Food & beverages (subject to conditions)
- Personal vehicle expenses
- Travel benefits to employees
- Configure appropriately to block ITC

RCM on Expenses:
- Unregistered vendor services
- Certain specified services
- Import of services
- Mark categories accordingly

BEST PRACTICES:
