TDS MASTER CONFIGURATION

Purpose: Configure Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) rules and rates.

QUICK ACCESS:
- [CREATE:TDS_MASTER] - Create a new TDS Master record
- [LIST:TDS_MASTER] - View all TDS Master records

Navigation Path: AG Taxation > Master Data > TDS Master > New

MANDATORY FIELDS:

1. Name: TDS type description (e.g., "Payment to Contractors (Company)")
2. Section: IT Act section (e.g., 194C)
3. TDS Type: Category
4. Rate: Standard rate (e.g., 2.0%)
5. TDS Account: Liability account (e.g., "2190 Tax Liability")
6. TDS Item: Payable item (e.g., "TDS Payable")
7. TDS Threshold: Single transaction limit (e.g., 30,000)
8. Cumulative Threshold: Annual limit
9. Assessee Code: Company/Individual
10. Empty PAN: Higher rate for non-PAN holders (e.g., 20.0%)
11. Payment Code: Section code
12. 206AB Rate: Special rate for non-filers
13. 206AB Higher Rate: Increased rate (e.g., 5.0%)

KEY FEATURE: 
All parameters defined here auto-populate on vendor bills when TDS is applied.

COMMON TDS SECTIONS:

Section 194C - Payment to Contractors:
- Company: 2%
- Individual/HUF: 1%
- Threshold: ₹30,000 (single), ₹1,00,000 (annual)

Section 194J - Professional Services:
- Rate: 10%
- Threshold: ₹30,000
- Applies to: Technical, professional, royalty

Section 194H - Commission:
- Rate: 5%
- Threshold: ₹15,000
- Applies to: Commission/brokerage

Section 194I - Rent:
- Plant & Machinery: 2%
- Land & Building: 10%
- Threshold: ₹2,40,000

Section 194A - Interest:
- Banks: 10%
- Others: 10%
- Threshold: ₹40,000 (others), ₹50,000 (senior citizens)

Section 194Q - Purchase of Goods:
- Rate: 0.1%
- Threshold: ₹50,00,000 (annual)

THRESHOLD TYPES:

Single Transaction Threshold:
- Applied per transaction
- If single bill exceeds limit, TDS applies

Cumulative Threshold:
- Applied annually per vendor
- Tracks total payments across financial year
- TDS applies when cumulative crosses limit

SPECIAL PROVISIONS:

Empty PAN Rate:
- Higher rate when vendor has no PAN
- Typically 20% for most sections
- Configured in "Empty PAN" field

206AB - Non-filer Provisions:
- Higher rate for non-ITR filers
- Rate: Higher of (normal × 2) or specified rate
- Applies if vendor didn't file ITR in last 2 years

ASSESSEE CODE:

Company (C):
- Registered companies
- Rate may differ from individuals

Individual/HUF (I):
- Individuals and Hindu Undivided Families
- Generally lower rates for contractors

CONFIGURATION EXAMPLE:

TDS Master: Payment to Contractors (Company)
├── Name: Payment to Contractors (Company)
├── Section: 194C
├── TDS Type: Contractor
├── Rate: 2.0%
├── TDS Account: 2190 TDS Liability
├── TDS Item: TDS Payable
├── TDS Threshold: 30,000
├── Cumulative Threshold: 100,000
├── Assessee Code: Company
├── Empty PAN: 20.0%
├── Payment Code: C01
├── 206AB Rate: 5.0%
└── 206AB Higher Rate: 5.0%

BEST PRACTICES:

- Create separate TDS masters for each section
- Configure different rates for Company vs Individual
- Set accurate thresholds as per IT Act
- Update rates when government revises them
- Test TDS calculation on sample bills
- Document TDS types for user reference
- Regular review of 206AB applicability

IMPORTANT NOTES:

- TDS Master must be created before vendor mapping
- Rates and thresholds change periodically - stay updated
- System auto-calculates based on configured parameters
- TDS cannot be applied if not configured in master
- Proper setup ensures compliance and accurate deduction
- Threshold validation happens automatically
