AG REPORTS (GSTR-1, GSTR-2, GSTR-3B)

Purpose: Generate statutory GST returns and compliance reports.

Navigation Path: AG Taxation > AG Reports

GSTR-1 REPORT (Sales Return):

Navigation: AG Taxation > AG Reports > AGTAX GSTR-1 Report

Report Sections:
- B2B: Business to Business (registered customers)
- B2CL: Business to Consumer Large (> ₹2.5 lakh)
- B2CS: Business to Consumer Small (< ₹2.5 lakh)
- CDNR: Credit/Debit Notes Registered
- CDNUR: Credit/Debit Notes Unregistered
- EXP: Export transactions
- HSN: HSN-wise summary

Filter Options:
- Customer: Specific customer
- Location: Company location
- GST Type: Intra-state/Inter-state/Export
- Subsidiary: Entity
- From Date: Start date
- To Date: End date
- Select Report: Choose specific section or ALL

Report Columns:
- GSTIN/UIN of Recipient
- Recipient Name
- Invoice Number
- Invoice Date
- Invoice Value
- Place of Supply
- Reverse Charge (Y/N)
- Invoice Type
- E-commerce GSTIN
- Rate %
- Taxable Value
- IGST Value
- CGST Value
- SGST Value
- Cess Amount

Actions:
- Click "Search" to generate
- Click "Export" for Excel download

GSTR-2 REPORT (Purchase Return):

Navigation: AG Taxation > AG Reports > AGTAX_GSTR_2_Report

Report Sections:
- B2B: Business to Business purchases
- B2BUR: Business to Business Unregistered
- IMPS: Import of Services
- IMPG: Import of Goods
- HSN: HSN-wise summary
- CDNR: Credit/Debit Notes Registered
- CDNUR: Credit/Debit Notes Unregistered

Filter Options:
- Vendor: Specific vendor
- Location: Company location
- GST Type: Purchase classification
- Subsidiary: Entity
- From Date: Start date
- To Date: End date
- Select Report: Choose section or ALL

Report Columns:
- GSTIN of Supplier
- PAN No
- Trade/Legal Name
- Invoice Number
- Invoice Type
- Invoice Date
- Invoice Value
- Place of Supply
- Supply attract Reverse Charge (Y/N)
- Rate %
- Taxable Value
- IGST Amount
- CGST Amount
- SGST Amount
- Cess Amount
- E-commerce GSTIN

Actions:
- Click "Search" to generate
- Click "Export" for Excel download

GSTR-3B REPORT (Monthly Summary Return):

Navigation: AG Taxation > AG Reports > AGTAX GSTR-3B Report

Report Sections:

1. Outward Supplies:
   - Taxable outward supplies
   - Zero-rated supplies
   - Exempted supplies
   - Non-GST outward supplies

2. ITC (Input Tax Credit) Available:
   - Imports of Goods
   - Imports of Services
   - Inward supplies on reverse charge
   - All other ITC
   - From supplier under composition scheme

3. ITC Reversed:
   - As per rules 38, 42, 43
   - Others

4. Ineligible ITC:
   - As per Section 17(5)
   - Others

5. Tax Liability:
   - CGST payable
   - SGST payable
   - IGST payable
   - Cess payable

6. Interest & Late Fee:
   - If applicable

Filter Options:
- Date: From date
- Date: To date
- Subsidiary: Select entity
- GSTIN: Auto-populated
- Place of Supply: State

Actions:
- Enter date range
- Click "Search"
- Review auto-calculated figures
- Click "Export" for Excel format

KEY FEATURES:

Auto-Calculation:
- All GSTR-3B sections auto-calculated
- Reconciliation with GSTR-1 and GSTR-2
- Ineligible ITC properly reflected
- Ready for government portal upload

Data Accuracy:
- Real-time calculation
- Transaction-level tracking
- Automatic updates
- Audit trail maintained

Compliance:
- Monthly filing (for most)
- Quarterly filing (for small taxpayers)
- Due dates: 20th of next month
- Late filing penalties apply

RECONCILIATION PROCESS:

GSTR-1 vs GSTR-3B:
- Outward supplies should match
- Verify taxable value
- Check tax amounts
- Resolve discrepancies

GSTR-2 vs GSTR-3B:
- ITC claimed should match
- Verify purchase values
- Check input credit
- Resolve mismatches

FILING WORKFLOW:

1. Generate Reports:
   - GSTR-1 for sales
   - GSTR-2 for purchases
   - GSTR-3B for summary

2. Review Data:
   - Check completeness
   - Verify accuracy
   - Reconcile sections
   - Resolve errors

3. Export Reports:
   - Excel format
   - Ready for upload
   - Government portal

4. Upload to Portal:
   - GST portal login
   - Upload JSON/Excel
   - Validate data
   - Submit returns

5. Payment:
   - Calculate liability
   - Generate challan
   - Make payment
   - Update records

IMPORTANT NOTES:
- Run reports monthly/quarterly
- Verify data before filing
- Keep export files for records
- Reconcile with books
- File within due dates
- Maintain documentation
- Critical for GST compliance
